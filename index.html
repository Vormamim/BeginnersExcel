💡 What is Excel?

Excel is a spreadsheet program made by Microsoft. People use it every day to:

Track scores and grades
Manage money and budgets
Create charts and graphs
Organize lists and data

Whether you want to track your gaming scores, plan a party, or organize your sports team stats — Excel can do it all!
